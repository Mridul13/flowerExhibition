Flowers Description
Agapanthus
These showy flowers are produced in colors such as blue and purple.
Cape Leadwort
Produces starry sky blue flowers.
Daisy
These spread and come in a variety of colors, such as white and pink, or even bicolored.
Kaffir Lily
Bell-shaped and orange in color, marked with yellow.
Lunaria
Blossoms are purple or white.
Starflower
Flowers are pure white stars.
